Lemon Blueberry Cake

Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Preparation

Step 1: Mix the Batter

Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add lemon zest and milk.

Step 2: Add Dry Ingredients

In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Slowly fold into the wet mixture until just combined. Gently add blueberries.

Step 3: Bake

Pour batter into a greased 9-inch pan. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 40–45 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool before serving.
